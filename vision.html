Vision of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Kalam's Vision for India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam dreamed of transforming India into a developed nation. His vision, famously known as "Vision 2020," focused on making India economically and technologically advanced while eradicating poverty and illiteracy.

  • Developing advanced infrastructure, including transportation, energy, and communication.
  • Emphasizing education and skill development to empower youth.
  • Strengthening the agricultural sector to ensure food security and sustainable growth.
  • Promoting scientific innovation and research for technological self-reliance.
  • Encouraging industrial growth to create job opportunities.
  • Ensuring affordable healthcare and access to clean water for all citizens.
