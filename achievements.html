Achievements of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Highlights of Achievements

  • Developed India's first indigenous Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
  • Played a pivotal role in the development of ballistic missiles like Agni and Prithvi.
  • Contributed significantly to India's nuclear tests at Pokhran in 1998.
  • Laid the foundation for the Indian space program at ISRO.
  • Received India's highest civilian honor, the Bharat Ratna, in 1997.
  • Inspired millions with his vision of a developed India through "Vision 2020."
  • Authored several books, including "Wings of Fire," which chronicles his life journey.
  • Served as the 11th President of India, earning the title "People's President."
