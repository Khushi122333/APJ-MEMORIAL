About Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, known as the 'Missile Man of India', was a visionary scientist, an inspiring leader, and the 11th President of India. He dedicated his life to education, innovation, and uplifting the youth of India. His journey from Rameswaram to Rashtrapati Bhavan is a testament to hard work, perseverance, and selfless service.

He played pivotal roles in the development of India's civilian space program and military missile systems. His work earned him the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.
